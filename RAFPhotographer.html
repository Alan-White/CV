RAF Photographer 1989 - 1994.

  • RAF Leuchars, 1990 - 1994. As part of a team of seven in the photographic section, I was responsible for taking, processing and printing PR and technical photographs.
    The photographic jobs included; parades, large group photo's, damaged aircraft, crashed aircraft, victims of assault, air to ground and air to air photography and VIP visits (Princess Diana, Princess Anne, MP's, the All Blacks, Scotland Rugby team and senior Forces Officers).
    As well as winning photographic awards as a section, I was personally awarded the following:
    • Winner, RAF Strike Command Technical Photography Competition 1991 - Black and White Portfolio Category.
    • 2nd Place, RAF Strike Command Technical Photography Competition 1992 - Individual Black and White Photograph Category.
  • RAF Cosford, 1990. Joint Services School of Photography. An intense six month course covering PR, technical and architectural photography, developing and printing.
  • RAF Swinderby, 1989. Basic training which incuded; marching, fitness, weapons, nuclear, biological and chemical defence and a lot of cleaning.
