Youth Expedition Leader

  • Peru 2006: Voluntary position as a mountain leader with BSES Expeditions. During this seven week expedition I led a group of ten teenagers for six weeks in a remote mountainous area. We trekked on snow and ice up to 5500m. We also assisted scientists conducting ornithology research.
  • Kyrgyzstan 1999: Voluntary position as a geologist/mountain leader with BSES Expeditions. During this seven week expedition I organised a geology mapping project for twelve teenagers and co-led a trek up the Inylcheck Glacier to the base camp for Kahn Tengri. The other leader became ill and we had to arrange a helicopter rescue.
  • India 1997: Voluntary position as an expedition leader with World Challenge Expeditions. During this four week expedition I led a group of twelve teenage schoolgirls on a trek which involved snow and ice at 4,750m. We also met local children and painted their primary school.
